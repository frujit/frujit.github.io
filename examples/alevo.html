Alevo.

What is Alevo?

This is a game created on the Roblox engine. In this you are to become a soldier who protects residents against hostile experiments on resurrecting people. Otherwise called Zombie. You can also be a green Zombie whose goal is to bring more people to your side.

"This game is available on all platforms thanks to the Roblox engine and launcher."

-Wiktor W.

What exactly can you do?

On the map you will find different forms of hiding places and the like. If you love space, then there will be space for you. In Alevo, you'll find Saturn, Earth and the Moon.

You can also release a robot with artificial intelligence on the map. He has his tactics, straged and powerful weapons. You can test yourself in cunning. In the game you will also find an audio chat, thanks to which when writing on a regular chat phrases such as boo, lol, tada and the like, each player will hear the sound of boo, laughter or tada.

When you are a soldier, you have many helpers in the form of policemen, SWAT, Larry, Steve and Josh. Be careful. Zombies can infect your helpers! But when you picked zombies on your team, you will find a strong zombie, a lumberjack zombie and ordinary zombies. Throughout the map, various machines are scattered, such as: trucks, planes, helicopters, cars, excavators, rockets and turrets. At the start, no matter if you are a zombie or a soldier, you have a number of weapons: RPG-7, rocket launcher, Ray Gun, PR-87 and many more.

Play on all devices!
